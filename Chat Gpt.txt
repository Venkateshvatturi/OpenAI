To import the Pega ChatGPT application, please follow the steps below:

1.Log in to Pega dev studio.
2.Click on "Configure" on the header session.
3.Go to "Application" -> "Distribution" -> "Import."
4.Import the Jar file.
5.After the Jar import is completed, close the tab and click on "Launch Portal."
6.Click on "Create" and launch the Chat GPT Case.

With Pega ChatGPT, you can ask Open AI any questions you want.
